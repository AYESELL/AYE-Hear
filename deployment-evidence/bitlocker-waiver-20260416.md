confirmed absence of encryption on a production deployment target.

---

## Risk Context

### Why this Waiver is Justified

1. **SAM_ULTRA is the development workstation, not the GA deployment target.**
   The AYE Hear V1 Deployment Guide specifies that target machines are
   end-user Windows 10/11 Pro or Enterprise hosts. BitLocker enablement
   on SAM_ULTRA is outside the deployment scope.

2. **manage-bde requires elevation.** Running the script without local
   Administrator rights produces a misleading FAIL verdict. The actual BitLocker
   state on SAM_ULTRA cannot be determined without elevation in the current
   shell context.

3. **Compensating controls are in place** (see section below) that reduce the
   residual risk to an acceptable level for Phase-1 release.
